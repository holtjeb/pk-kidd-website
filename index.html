Serving Your Kids

Our mission here at P.K. Kidd is to give our kids the skills they need to have a successful education now and in the future. We take pride in our preschool and the way we choose to operate. Our kidds truly come first. We choose to have smaller class sizes in order to give the children more one on one attention. At P.K. Kidd, we laugh, we learn, but above all we love!

An Experience Like No Other!

We make it our goal to teach your kids first, but we find it just as important for them to have fun and be creative! Everyday, we have a craft which the student can make on their own. We show them how to make all kinds of interesting crafts dealing with our weekly letter, holidays, and more!
